Entities:

Player - 2 players (1 human vs 1 human), (1 human vs CPU), (CPU vs CPU)?
Deck - contains 54 cards (x2 jokers, 13 cards x 4 suits)
Card - has a Rank and a Suit
Suit - hearts (red), diamonds (red), spades (black), clubs (black)
Turn - one player draws card

Game start:

Randomly determine who goes first. Deck is shuffled and dealt to each player (27 cards per player)
1. Player 1 draws card
2. Player 2 draws card
3. Determine which card is higher
4. Player with higher card gets opposing players card
5. If both cards drawn are equal players draw one card face down, and a second card face up and the higher face cards wins
6. Repeat 1 - 5 until one player runs out of cards
